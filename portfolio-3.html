Pet Adoption Research durring a Pandemic

More than Ever, People Want to Adopt Animals

"In February 2020, Google searches around adopting a pet surged about 335%" - SEMrush, Data and Trends Analytics Company

Project Overview

How do people feel about virtual pet adoptions with and without the threat of covid-19? This research asks partners in the adoption process their point of view on virtual and in-person pet adoptions to understand the adoption process and to find points of frustration and opportunities for innovation.

Takeaway

Balancing human and animal centered, service design is needed to improve trust. Pet adoption services should be designed for people to understand animals.
